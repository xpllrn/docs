## How to purchase

<Steps>
  <Step title="Join the support server">
    Open a ticket in our [Support Server](https://discord.gg/q3e53k4qKQ).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a plan">
    Pick **Personal Premium**, **Server Premium**, or a **Private Instance** for your server or account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete payment">
    Pay via **PayPal**, **Bank Transfer**, or **Bitcoin / Ethereum / Litecoin**. Our team will activate your subscription or instance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Transfer anytime">
    You can **transfer** Server Premium or a Private Instance to a different Discord server by opening another ticket.
  </Step>
</Steps>
